Hello, I'm Jasper Latour

I am 20 Years old and live in Helmond, the Netherlands. I still live at home with my parents, brother and sister.

I'm currently studying software development at Fontys University in Eindhoven. My studies mainly focus on developing software with hardware in mind, such as programming Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and other development boards.

In my free time, I enjoy 3D printing useful objects as well as undertaking projects. Additionally, I am a group leader for kids between the ages of 10 and 14 in Scouting. This involves activities every Friday evening and around four weekends per year.
